Frequently Asked Questions

1. What products does MKJ Electronics offer?

We offer a variety of electronics including laptops, smartphones, smart TVs, and digital cameras from trusted brands.

2. How do I place an order?

Simply browse our product pages, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive confirmation via email once the purchase is complete.

3. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and secure bank transfers. More payment options coming soon.

4. Can I return a product?

Yes, returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in original condition and packaging. Visit our Returns Policy for more details.

5. Do you offer warranties?

Yes, most products come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranties are also available at checkout for select products.

6. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email. You can also view order status in your account dashboard.

7. How do I contact customer service?

You can reach us via our Contact page or email us directly at support@mkjelectronics.com.

8. Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We plan to offer international shipping soon.

9. Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to ensure your data and transactions are protected at all times.

10. Can I cancel or change my order?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can contact us to modify or cancel it. Once shipped, you'll need to follow our return process.
