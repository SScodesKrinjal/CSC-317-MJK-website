About MKJ Electronics

MKJ Electronics was founded by three passionate tech enthusiasts: Momina, Joshua, and Krinjal. We started our journey in 2025 with one goal: to create a tech company that not only delivers high-quality electronics but also cares deeply about its customers and their needs.

The name "MKJ" represents the initials of the three founders: Momina, Krinjal, and Joshua, symbolizing our united vision to bring innovative electronics into the hands of everyday people.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to make cutting-edge technology affordable, accessible, and enjoyable for all. Whether it's a smartphone, laptop, or smart home device, MKJ Electronics is here to help you embrace the future of technology.
